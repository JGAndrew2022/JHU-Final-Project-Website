Our Story

We saw an opportunity to make a difference in dog daycare and boarding in Colorado and we went for it! In 2004 we opened "Playful Paws Denver", which is Denver's largest indoor and outdoor canine activity center. We never rest, and in 2006 we opened "Playful Paws COS South". Almost as big as our Denver location, this became our answer for dogs deprived of a great place to play in Southern Colorado Springs. With indoor and outdoor agility areas. Playful Paws COS serves proudly as a center of canine activity and soon, feline boarding. "Playful Paws COS North" and "Playful Paws Fort Collins" soon followed. Although slightly smaller that COS South and Denver, these facilities were still far from lacking, being some of the largest in their respective cities. Playful Paws continues to work to make their facilities the very best possible. We are even working to introduce feline boarding! In addition, we have accessory stores at all of our locations, so you'll never be out of your dog's favorite items.

You can be rest assured when you bring your dog to Playful Paws for daycare services while you're at work. Constantly supervised by our professionals, your dogs will enjoy scheduled exercise activities and lots and lots of affection. We have two daycare areas to accommodate various sizes of dogs. Not to mention our boarding where your furry friend can get luxury beds and treatment while your on vacation. We'll make sure your dog is always happy with Playful Paws.
